Successful Projects Funded Through Green Square Capital

Project: Old Cotton Factory Works, 117 Green Street, Manchester

Description:

A former commercial building transformed into four modern, fully refurbished residential flats. This project used investor funding to convert underutilised space into high-quality rental accommodation, increasing the property’s value and generating ongoing rental income for both investors and the property owner.

Project: Bedford Police Station, 99b Walkway, Bristol

Description:

A former police station transformed into residential flats, with a fully refurbished gym in the basement exclusively for residents. Investor funding enabled the conversion of the building into high-quality rental accommodation, boosting the property’s value and generating long-term rental income for both investors and the property owner.

Project: The Willow House, 22 Meadow Lane, Leeds

Description:

A derelict property transformed into a modern, comfortable family home. Investor funding facilitated the full renovation, turning an underutilised building into a high-quality, long-term rental. The project increased the property’s market value while providing investors with a share of future rental income or capital growth.

Project: Hawthorne Manor, 15 Kingsway, Oxford

Description:

A historic manor house converted into modern serviced offices. Investor funding enabled the restoration of the building, preserving its character while providing flexible office space for small businesses. The project enhanced the property’s value and created a consistent long-term income stream for both investors and the property owner.
